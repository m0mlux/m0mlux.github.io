Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you offer?

I offer web development services, mobile application creation, Discord bot development, and digital technology consulting.

How does a project work?

Each project begins with a discussion phase to understand your needs, followed by a detailed proposal. After validation, development begins with regular check-ins to ensure the project meets your expectations.

What are your delivery timeframes?

Timeframes vary depending on project complexity. A simple showcase site might take 2-3 weeks, while a complete application could require 2-3 months. I'll always provide you with an accurate estimate before starting.

Do you offer after-sales support?

Yes, all my projects include a support period after delivery. Maintenance contracts are also available for longer-term support.
